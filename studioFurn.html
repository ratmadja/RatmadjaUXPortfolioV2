Furniture Design

I have a fascination with off-the-shelf hardware pieces and enjoy adapting them for a different purpose than what they were originally made for. Here is a series of floral vases made of copper piping, resin, and glass. Each flower vase is designed to highlight the beauty of a single flower. It elevates the flower head to be at the eye-level of a person who is sitting on the table.
