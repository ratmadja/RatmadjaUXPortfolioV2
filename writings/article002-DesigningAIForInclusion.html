Designing AI for Social Inclusion

When AI and ethics intersect in a design endeavor

October 23, 2020

When humans create an intelligent, AI-powered entity in their own image—it is highly likely that the AI entity will inherit the best, and the worst, of the humans who created it.

In this article, I write about some practical steps that the Designers and Builders of AI can take in order to enable greater social inclusion to be codified into the very natures of the AI entities that they will bring into the world.
